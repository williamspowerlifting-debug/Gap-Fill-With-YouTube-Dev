📖 How to Use

Create and share gap-fill listening exercises quickly — upload a video, edit gaps, and save or share with students.

1. Upload a video

Drag and drop or click. Any common format works.

2. Wait for transcript

The transcript appears automatically — no button to click.

3. Edit gaps

  • Click any word to make it a gap (turns yellow).
  • Click a yellow word to remove the gap.
  • "✏️ Edit Text" button — activates edit mode. Then double-click any word (or double-tap on mobile) to edit it inline. Gap words stay highlighted. Editing a gap word updates its answer automatically. Clearing a word removes it and its gap. Press Enter to confirm or Esc to cancel.

4. Generate Gaps

AI fills in extra gaps based on the level you choose.

5. Share with students

  • Preview — See what students will see.
  • Student Link — Copy and send. No signup needed.
  • Save Exercise — Saves to your Saved Exercises list on this browser. Sign in with Google to sync your exercises across devices.

My Activities

Click 📚 My Activities at any time to see all activities you've created. You can open them, copy student links, or delete them.

How students use it

  1. The video plays the first sentence automatically.
  2. They type the missing words.
  3. They click ✅ Submit — green ✓ for correct, red ✗ for wrong.
  4. The next sentence loads after 6 seconds.
  5. At the end, they see their score.
